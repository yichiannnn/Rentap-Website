Our Story

The Legend of Rentap

Rentap was a legendary Iban warrior from Sarawak who led a fierce resistance against Brooke Colonial rule in the mid-19th century. His name means "Shaker of Hearts" — or "Earth Shaker."

After witnessing the inequality of his people and the disregard for their customs, he led a revolution against the oppressors. History remembers him as a symbol of Indigenous resistance and undying pride.

RENTAP XVII carries that spirit into Germany. It is not merely a sports tournament — it is a declaration that the Malaysian community here is still alive, still fighting, still proud.

"Let RENTAP XVII be a living tribute to that spirit — a reminder of pride, resilience, courage, and the will to shake the hearts of the people."

More Than Sports

Culture & Community

Food Bazaar

An authentic Malaysian street food bazaar runs alongside the tournament all weekend. Nasi lemak, satay, teh tarik, and more — alongside local German classics.

Malam Kebudayaan

Saturday night becomes a cultural showcase — traditional Malaysian performances, live music, dancing, and community celebration running until 10 PM.

The Society

RENTAP is organised by a registered Malaysian community e.V. in Germany. We welcome permanent members who share our love for culture and community spirit beyond the event.
